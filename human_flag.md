# Human Flag Training
## Exercises:
- 45 degree straddle flag
- 45 degree normal flag
- flag plank
- straddle flag plank
- kick to flag
- kick to straddle flag
- band assisted flag
- band assisted straddle flag

## Trainingsplan:
- 3*5-10 reps kick to straddle flag
- 3*5-10 reps kick to flag
- 3*15s straddle flag plank
- 3*15s flag plank
